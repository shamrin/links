youtube-dl - download videos from YouTube

December 16 2011, 5:46 AM  by Alexey Shamrin

youtube-dl is a small command-line program to download videos from YouTube.com and a few more sites.

Here's how to download video suitable for iPhone:

$ youtube-dl -l -f 18 <youtube-ID-or-link>

-l - nice file name

-f 18 - optimal iPhone format
