Anti-piracy strategy

April 18 2010, 6:07 AM  by Alexey Shamrin
HN comment by chaosmachine (on MMO dev fools pirates with torrent data ‘leak’ thread):

I always thought it would be a good anti-piracy strategy to seed trackers with your own subtly broken cracks (a crack that intentionally stops working after 2 weeks, for example). The key is to upload your fake crack before anyone gets around to making a real one.
