On faxing, scanning, printing and signing

May 4 2010, 6:15 AM  by Alexey Shamrin
The big question - why can't fax machine die? - opened a discussion on Hacker News. After lot's clicking I ended with:
  • Description of a nice iPhone application to scan documents - Scanner Pro. Cool idea to define page boundaries to fix distortions and scale!
  • Two interesting web apps for easy e-signing of documents - RightSignature (founded by HNer) and EchoSign. With two more competitors (haven't reviewed them myself) - Agree 2 and SignatureLink.
